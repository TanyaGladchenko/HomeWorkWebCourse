This political map is designed to show governmental boundaries of countries and states,
the location of some major cities, as well as significant bodies of water.
Bright colors are used here to help you (the viewer) find the borders, and as you can see,
most in the Caribbean and Europe are very hard to distinguish.

There are 195 countries in the world today. See also: List of countries

Of the 195 countries in the world:

  • 54 countries are in Africa
  • 48 in Asia
  • 44 in Europe
  • 33 in Latin America and the Caribbean
  • 14 in Oceania
  • 2 in Northern America
